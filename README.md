# Disneydex Movie Tracker

## 📖 Overview
**Disneydex Movie Tracker** is a native Android application designed for fans of the expansive Walt Disney Company universe. Drawing inspiration from major streaming platforms, this app provides a centralized hub to explore the movieographies of major franchises—including Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and Avatar. 

Beyond simply browsing release dates and sagas, the app serves as a personal collection manager and a community hub. Users can track their watching progress, manage their physical media collections (DVD/Blu-ray), and even discover other users who are looking to trade or get rid of movies they own.

## ✨ Features

* **Secure Authentication:** Full user account management (Login, Registration, and Profile setup) powered by Firebase Authentication.
* **Explore Universes:** Browse movies categorized by major cinematic universes (Marvel, Disney, Pixar, Star Wars, Avatar) and specific sub-categories (e.g., The Skywalker Saga).
* **Movie Details:** View comprehensive lists of movies along with their original theatrical release dates.
* **Collection & Watchlist Management:** Users can interact with any movie and mark it with customized statuses:
    * ✅ *Watched*
    * 👀 *Want to watch*
    * 💿 *Own on DVD / Blu-Ray*
    * 📦 *Want to get rid of*
* **Community Trading Hub:** On individual movie pages, view a dynamic list of other users who currently own the physical copy and have marked it as "Want to get rid of."
* **Profile Dashboard:** A dedicated space for users to manage their personal list of owned movies and update their statuses at any time.
* **Real-Time Data:** Fully dynamic and connected to Firebase Realtime Database, ensuring that collection updates and community statuses are synced instantly.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Platform:** Android Native (Kotlin)
* **Backend:** Firebase Realtime Database
* **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication (Email/Password)
